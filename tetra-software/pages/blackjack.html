Blackjack

Blackjack is a fast and complete game of Blackjack.

Install:

Update:

How to install:

Step 1: Download Blackjack by pressing the "Download Blackjack Executable" button above.

Step 2: Extract "blackjack.zip" into a folder of your choice.

Step 3: Follow the instructions in the file "INSTALL.TXT".

How to update:

Step 1: Download the latest patch by pressing the "Download Blackjack Update" button above.

Step 2: Copy "blackjack.patch" into your Blackjack directoty and follow the instructions in the "INSTALL.TXT" file.
